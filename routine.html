My Current Hair Care Routine

What I Eat for Healthy Hair

Eating mindfully has been key to my hair health. These plates include lots of colorful vegetables and greens, balancing carbs with fiber and protein to nourish my body from within.

Daily Ritual – Nourish from Within

Feeding your body with wholesome, nutrient-rich foods is key to supporting healthy hair growth. Here’s how I eat to support my hair daily:

  • Include soaked nuts and seeds like almonds, walnuts, and chia for essential fatty acids.
  • Eat seasonal vegetables such as white pumpkin, spinach, banana stem, and curry leaves for iron, fiber, and hydration.
  • Consume protein-rich foods like lentils, dal, boiled eggs, or legumes regularly.
  • Add healthy fats from ghee, cold-pressed oils, and Brazil nuts to support scalp health.
  • Incorporate natural sources of vitamin C like amla, figs, and prunes for collagen production and hair strength.
  • Stay hydrated with herbal teas, coconut water, or plain water throughout the day.
I include fruits, nuts, seeds, and herbs in my daily diet to nourish my hair from within.

Hair Care Philosophy

I believe hair care is an art that thrives on consistency, quality ingredients, and mindful rituals. I focus on plant-based products that are seasonal, handcrafted, and rooted in tradition.

Weekly Ritual – Cleanse

Product: Back to roots Hair Cleanser
Frequency: Every 3 days
How: Mixed with warm water, massaged onto scalp, and rinsed.

Weekly Ritual – Oil

Product: Back to Roots Hair Oil
Frequency: Twice a week
How: Warmed and massaged into scalp. Left it for 1-3 hours and washed with Back to roots Hair Cleanser.

The oil deeply nourishes my roots and relaxes my stressed body as well. The cleanser is gentle, chemical-free, and maintains scalp health. Together, they keep my hair soft, manageable, and strong.
